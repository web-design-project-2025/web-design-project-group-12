ABOUT US AT THE FINAL CUT

At The Final Cut, we're three students from Jönköping University studying graphic design and web development. As movie lovers, we often found it frustrating to scroll through cluttered movie websites, unsure of what to watch next. So, we decided to create our own platform — one that makes discovering and sharing great films simple and enjoyable.

On The Final Cut, users can review movies, rate them, and give recommendations to others. We've also added a watchlist feature, so you can easily save movies you want to see — or revisit the ones you loved. Our goal is to build a clean, community-driven space for movie fans to connect and help each other find their next favorite film.
