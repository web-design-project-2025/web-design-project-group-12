STEP 1 - WATCH A MOVIE

The first step in using the website is to select a movie of your choice and watch it. There are no restrictions on genre or category—you are free to choose any film that interests you.

STEP 2 - WRITE A REVIEW

Once you’ve finished watching a movie, it’s time to write a review and share your honest opinion. On Final Cut, there are three different ways to submit a review: Via the Navigation Bar: Click on the “Review” option in the top navigation bar. This will take you directly to the review submission page. Through the Movie Page: Use the search bar or browse by category to find the movie you watched. Once you’ve located the film, click on it to open the detail page—there, you’ll find an option to write a review. From Your Profile: Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of the site to access your profile page. There, you’ll see a button labeled “Write a New Review.” Follow the on-screen instructions on the review page to complete and submit your review.
