STEP 1 - WATCH A MOVIE

The first step in using the website is to select a movie of your choice and watch it. There are no restrictions on genre or category—you are free to choose any film that interests you.

STEP 2 - WRITE A REVIEW

Once you’ve finished watching a movie, it’s time to write a review and share your honest opinion. You can do this through the navigation bar, a detail page on any movie or through your profile.

STEP 3 - UPLOAD YOUR REVIEW

Once you've completed your review, simply post it. Your review will appear both on the movie’s detail page and in the “Your Reviews” section of your profile. You can edit or update your reviews at any time, ensuring your opinions stay current.
